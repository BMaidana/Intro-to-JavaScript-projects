Problem Statement

Write a program that will prompt a user for a student’s name and the student’s exam score out of 100. Your program will match the exam score to a letter grade and print the student’s name, score, and grade to the page. Calculate the letter grade from this table. Remember that exam scores may have decimal portions, and are not just whole numbers. Due to the potential of extra credit, and the possibility of exam score curving, the maximum possible score is not 100.

Users should receive an error message if the input is less than zero or not numeric.

Exam Score Assigned Grade
90 and above A
80 to less than 90 B
70 to less than 80 C
60 to less than 70 D
below 60 F
